Creator

Kamel Dela Adjei is a Final Year student pursuing a Bsc Digital Media Design degree in the University of Plymouth in the United Kingdom. A self-proclaimed AI activist who seeks to create more opportunities in AI for marginalized communities and make AI more inclusive and widespread especially for individuals in his home country, Ghana, West Africa. He is also an avid critic of the use of flawed predictive algorithms and supports the need for proper examinations conducted for these systems, as well as, analysing the algorithm's intepretation of training data .As a Black man, he finds it important to speak about these issues as technology continues to display similar biases that negative impact individuals from the same community as him.

"AI Available and Accessible for Everyone"
